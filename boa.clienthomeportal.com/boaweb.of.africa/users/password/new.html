Welcome to BOAweb!

Welcome to the BANK OF AFRICA BOAweb Internet Banking portal.

We are pleased to inform you that B-Web has changed to BOAweb, with a new look.

With BOAweb, you have access to the same features with enhanced security.

In case you activated the Multi-factor Authentification on your account, you should provide:
  • Your username
  • Your password
  • The code provided by Google authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator mobile application to log in.

To log in to the new BOAweb platform, make sure you have the following information ready:

  • Your current B-Web username
  • Your account number

Digipass has been replaced by Google Authenticator and Microsoft Authenticator. Depending on your mobile device, please download either of the two applications from your device application store.

For more details, please contact your BANK OF AFRICA branch.
